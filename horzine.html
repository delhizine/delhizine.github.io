Horzine Around

"Can you give me a quote to go with your zine?"

"CANNOT pick because there's these two that always go hand in hand like I think of one and the other one pops up immediately —

  1. 'Yet because I knew nothing, nothing was beneath me.'
    Pg 74 of Circe life-changing so simple like FUCK why did I not know this already — What an icon Madeline Miller.

  2. This one quote I read in a Joachim Trier interview where he is wuoting Kierkegaard and adding his own thing to it :
    'We can only understand our life backward, but we’re forced to live it forward. And I think that’s the confusion we all feel, is that we always learn too late. We go through things that are completely inexplicable and mysterious. And then years later, we realize.'

Use either or both. I think both is nicer."
