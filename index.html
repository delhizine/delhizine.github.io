What?

It's a zine collective of zine-ers who are currently zine-ing in Delhi. You might have stumbled onto us on @delhizine or on any one of our displays located in and around delhi. For the list of zines (and their printable copies) hosted by us click here.

But what's a zine??

You know what a magazine is? Well it's half that - a zine. It's anything and everything you can fit in the 8 page booklet made out of 1 A4 sheet - a story, your local news, the life updates of your favourite rock, everything that has and will rock your world. Learn how to make you own here.

Where can we pick up a copy?

We're planning to keep an updated list of pop-up displays on this site. For now it's upto your good luck to bump into anyone carrying the zines. The site is, as is obvious, under construction, so don't fret if the links don't work - just check back in a while :)

How do we get involved?

If you would like our patronage, mail us. If you find a cool leaf, mail us. If you would like to tell a secret, mail us. We are listening at delhizine at the rate gmail dot com.
