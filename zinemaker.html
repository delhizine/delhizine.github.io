DZ Zineinator v.0.0

Go Home

  1. Draw your zine. One page will be something around 7.4 cm wide and 10.5 cm tall. You can make the pages in any size though, just make sure the page height is roughly 1.414 times the width.
  2. Order the contents, all 6 pages of it. Click on "Choose Files" and select them in order (this is important). Then select the back cover and then the front cover.
  3. Select the DPI, or ignore, go right ahead and Generate the Zine and hit download.
  4. Tada!
  5. Didn't work on first try? It will eventually, feel free to mail us about bugs et al.
