Letters to my friends

Everything we learnt in school was useless. At least for me. I've learnt more from my mistakes than the formulas and formats. So I think making a zine based on letter writing was my way of finding a source to put this knowledge of class 10th English writing comprehension formats to use. But then again, I used none of that in the zine. So to answer the real question, why does this zine exist- Why? Because I was bored and I could.
